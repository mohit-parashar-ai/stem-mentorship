## 🕐 Time Zone Scheduling

### New Considerations

**United Kingdom (GMT/BST)**:
- 5 hours ahead of EST
- 8 hours ahead of PST
- Evening US time = Late night UK
- Morning UK time = Early morning US

**Australia (AEST/AEDT)**:
- 14-17 hours ahead of US
- When it's 9am in Sydney, it's 7pm (previous day) in NYC
- Best overlap: US late evening / Australia morning

### Recommended Scheduling

1. **North America** (USA, Canada): US business hours
2. **Europe** (UK): Early morning or evening US time
3. **Middle East** (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar): Afternoon US time
4. **Oceania** (Australia): Early morning or late evening US time

Consider offering flexible scheduling or different session blocks for different regions.
